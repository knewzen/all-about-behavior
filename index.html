Our mission

To create a challenging, rewarding, and supportive learning environment for children with Autism and their families using the principles of Applied Behavior Analysis to target skills that connect children to the world around them.

The AAB Difference

We know that you want the most compassionate and effective care possible to see your child/adolescent learn and grow.

We understand that as a parent of a child with Autism that you may experience information overload while coping with a diagnosis, which can be very emotional and overwhelming. We hope to alleviate some of that stress by providing you with research based methods while listening to your input and working to target goals in a way that works for your family and your child.

We start with a parent interview, rapport building, and an assessment process that allows us to get to know you and your child better. We then set individualized goals based off the assessments and your valuable input as parents. With this we can begin treatment using the principles of Applied Behavior Analysis in 1:1 therapy.

With this intensive approach, we can target a variety of goals and incorporate functional learning opportunities that can help your child/adolescent to have success in a variety of settings.
